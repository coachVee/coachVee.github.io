About The Founder

Driven by a deep passion for empowering people, Coach Vee is committed to building impactful training platforms that help aspiring professionals succeed in the financial services sector.

With over six years of experience in the Financial Services industry, she brings a strong combination of real-world regulatory knowledge, structured coaching, and learner-focused development. Her background as a Learning and Development Associate Specialist and Product Coach allows her to translate complex industry requirements into clear, practical learning experiences.

Beyond training content, Coach Vee is intentional about building supportive learning communities where students are guided, encouraged, and held accountable throughout their journey. She believes meaningful growth happens when knowledge, structure, and community come together—creating an environment where learners can confidently progress toward their career goals.

DISCLAIMER: Coach Vee provides coaching and tutoring services only. Coach Vee does not issue accredited qualifications or CPD points and is not accredited by the QCTO, SETA, or any statutory education or training authority.
